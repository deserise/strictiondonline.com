Healthy Food

Healthy eating, especially in the beginning, can be hard to stick to. Constructing a balanced meal, finding all the ingredients (nearby and for a reasonable price), and cooking it all is enough legwork to give up before you really begin.

The irony of the situation is that most of these hurdles will disappear the longer you practice clean eating; you'll find recipes you like, learn which stores carry certain ingredients, and learn how to manipulate them all to make something delicious quickly and efficiently.
